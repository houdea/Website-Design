Website Design Review

Apple

Apple has always had a impressive website that holds various aspects
most websites would love to have. It is a clean design and is aestheticly
pleasing to the eye. Something else I noticed was that at night the background
is balck while during the day it is white. I have not seen that before on a
website.

Nestle

For Nestle, I was impressed by its site's large amount of information
on various aspects of the company and its activity, even if the site is not
cutting edge. Other positives were its clean aesthetic, strong headlines and
well-chosen of hyperlinks. All-in-all, the website is well-done for a large
corporation.

Photos By Diane

This website I found just browsing around, I loved the simplistic
white nature of the website. The website uses a photo gallery that shows the
user this is what we want you to focus on. The colors contrast really well
together and the website is centered.
